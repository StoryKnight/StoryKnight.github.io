Incursion

Here's my WIP blurb:

Seventeen-year-old Maverick’s new life begins when the government decides he’s better off dead.

The catch: he can’t die. He’s a “crank”, a regenerative killer created by Omnia ©, a totalitarian state and bio-tech pioneer, as the ultimate soldier. Maverick’s never been popular, but his infamy worsens in the city when it’s clear that the brainwashed cranks do their job a little too well. In a post-utopian world where the population is dwindling and controversy is higher than ever, Maverick meets the rebels of Incursion who believe in a revolution — and cranks are very much involved.

Maverick’s meeting with Incursion puts him into the path of Aisha, a girl with a penchant for violence, a reckless plan, and enough firearms to topple the crank-centered government. Aisha coerces Maverick to join, and they head to Incursion’s half-headquarters, half-refuge front of Baskertown, the “city under the city”, where he begins to see Omnia’s lower-citizens in a different light. The people are experimented on and abused. Omnia is hated. Loyal cranks are churned out by the day. Maverick realizes that he must fight to destroy the government — even if it means taking down his own kind along the way.
